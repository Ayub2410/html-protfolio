Frequently Asked Questions

Booking

To make a booking on BayTravel.com, follow these simple steps:

  1. Use the search bar to find your desired destination and travel dates.
  2. Browse the available options and select your preferred accommodation or travel package.
  3. Click on the "Book Now" button and enter your personal and payment details.
  4. Review your booking information and confirm your reservation.
  5. You will receive a confirmation email with your booking details.

Yes, you can usually modify or cancel your booking, subject to the terms and conditions of the specific travel package or accommodation you have booked. To modify or cancel your booking, please refer to your booking confirmation email or contact our customer support team for assistance.
